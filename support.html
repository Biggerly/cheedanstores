Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?
Delivery within Anambra takes 1-2 business days. Other regions may take 3-5 business days.
What's your return policy?
Items can be returned within 7 days of delivery. Product must be unused and in original packaging.
How do I track my order?
Go to "My Orders" in your account to see real-time tracking information for your orders.
Do you deliver outside Anambra?
Yes! We deliver nationwide. Delivery fees vary by location and order value.
